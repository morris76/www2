CSCU Economic Impact

This report assesses the impact of Connecticut State Colleges and Universities (CSCU) on the state economy and the benefits generated by the institutions for students, taxpayers, and society. The results of this study show that CSCU creates a positive net impact on the state economy and generates a positive return on investment for students, taxpayers, and society.
