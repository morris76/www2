Supporting Education and Research

The CSCU Foundation, Inc. is a non-profit organization that promotes, encourages and assists education and research within the Connecticut State Colleges & Universities. The foundation solicits donations and accepts gifts, grants and bequests to support its work.

Barnard Awards

12 outstanding college seniors who attend Central, Eastern, Southern and Western Connecticut State Universities are honored each academic year by the CSUS Foundation during the annual Henry Barnard Distinguished Student Awards Banquet.

Scholarships

Scholarships are available for incoming freshman, transfer students from the community colleges to the universities and current university students. All scholarships are for residents of Connecticut and dependent on financial need.
