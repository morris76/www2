Make a Gift

Planned giving provides a way for donors to enjoy tax advantages and sometimes income benefits, while contributing to the future of CSUS and assisting students attending Central, Eastern, Southern and Western Connecticut State Universities.

A planned gift is often incorporated into estate and financial planning. There are several types of planned gifts that can benefit the CSUS Foundation, including bequests in a will or trust, retirement plan gifts, life insurance gifts, life income gifts, life income plans, retained life estates, and charitable lead trusts.

Endowments can be established for student scholarships, in accordance with specific criteria. An endowment fund preserves capital, offers long-term growth, keeps pace with or exceeds inflation, and can offer significant tax advantages to the donor.
