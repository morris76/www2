Preschool through 20 and Workforce Information Network

Linking data to improve education for students of all ages

What is P20 WIN?

P20 WIN securely links education and workforce data to provide answers to help improve education programs and workforce alignment.This enables CT to address questions such as:

  • What high school academic experiences are the best predictors of student's success?
  • Which indicators can be used to support students to be college and career ready?
  • Which certificates and degrees enable students to work in Connecticut?

CT agencies part of P20 WIN include:

  • State Department of Education (SDE)
  • Board of Regents for Higher Education (BOR)
  • University of Connecticut (UConn)
  • Department of Labor (DOL)
  • Connecticut Conference of Independent Colleges (CCIC)
  • Office of Early Childhood
  • Office of Policy and Management
