Preschool through 20 and Workforce Information Network

Improving education and employment opportunities for students through the secure sharing of data

What is P20 WIN?

P20 WIN is a network of CT agencies sharing secure data to learn and improve from education and employment outcomes in the state:

  • State Department of Education (SDE)
  • Board of Regents for Higher Education (BOR)
  • University of Connecticut (UConn)
  • Department of Labor (DOL)
  • Connecticut Conference of Independent Colleges (CCIC)
Leadership from each agency meets regularly to increase data interoperability for the benefit of the state.

Data Requests

Each data request requires substantial documentation and the execution of a data sharing agreement before any personally identifiable data can be shared or linked. Refer to the P20 WIN Data Request Management Procedure (pdf) for a description of the process for requesting data and descriptions of the required documentation.
