The department is responsible for all application development for the CT Community Colleges, including student registration, financial aid, fee assessment and billing, finance, employee and student payroll, and distance learning. This includes several interfaces to government and third-party agencies, such as the National Student Clearinghouse and the State's CORE-CT system.

Institutional Research

Powered by Evisions Argos, the BIS group develops and maintains a data warehouse resporting system that provides the Board of Regents Institutional Research group the raw data to analyze trends in the academic data.
Visit Evisions

Enterprise Resource Planning system - Banner

Powered by Ellucian's Banner enterprise system, the Business Information Systems department maintains and provides development support for the Student Information System, including admissions, financial aid, scheduling, registration, billing and refunds, grading and reporting.
